Page 03 — Margins

Three notes in the margin

The sketch style is a process made visible — every mark is deliberate.

Note 01

Doubled, wobbly lines

Every border is drawn twice, slightly offset and rotated — the imperfect overlap reads instantly as “by hand”.

Note 02

Crosshatch, not fill

Shading comes from diagonal pencil strokes layered into a hatch — never a flat solid block of colour.

Note 03

Handwriting & ink

Headlines and labels are handwritten; one steady ink-blue accent and a clean sans keep the body legible.
