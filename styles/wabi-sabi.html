The philosophy

Three quiet principles

Wabi-sabi is not a style applied — it is a posture of restraint and acceptance.

一

Embrace imperfection

No edge is perfectly true. Asymmetry and the hand-thrown wobble are kept, not corrected — the irregular is what feels alive.

二

Honour impermanence

Surfaces look weathered, worn soft by use. Where something cracks, the seam is filled with gold — the repair becomes the beauty.

三

Welcome the incomplete

Generous, quiet negative space. Muted earthen tone. Nothing shouts; the eye is given room to rest and to wander.
