Three sweet rules ✿

Kawaii is warmth — joy built from soft shapes and happy little faces.

♡
Rule 01

Round everything

Huge radii, pill shapes and circles. Not a single sharp corner — every edge is soft, pillowy and pettable.

☻
Rule 02

Give it a face

A smiley blob mascot, ^‿^ eyes and rosy blush cheeks turn plain components into friendly little characters.

✦
Rule 03

Bounce with joy

Springy, over-shooting motion and gentle wiggles on hover make every tap feel cheerful and alive.
