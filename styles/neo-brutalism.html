Three rules, one attitude

Neo-Brutalism is confidence — no apologies, no soft edges.

Rule 01

Fat black borders

Every box gets a thick, solid outline. Borders are not decoration — they are the structure that holds the whole look together.

Rule 02

Hard offset shadows

Shadows are solid blocks with zero blur, offset down-right. Press a control and it slams forward into its own shadow.

Rule 03

Loud clashing colour

Electric blue next to hot pink next to lime. The palette is meant to clash — flat, saturated, and impossible to ignore.
