Section 3 · 🔑 Decode

3 rules 2 🔓 the style

Rebus is a wink — meaning hidden one puzzle-piece deep.

1
Rule 01
🖼 + 🔤

Picture plus letter

Swap a syllable for an icon, a letter or a numeral. The reader's brain snaps the sounds together — "B" + 🐝-sound = the rebus click.

2
Rule 02
🧩 = 😄

Solving feels good

A tiny puzzle gives a tiny reward. Bright tiles, chunky outlines and a satisfying "pop" make every decode a small game-show moment.

3
Rule 03
🔑 = A11Y

Always give the key

A puzzle nobody can solve is just noise. Plain labels and ARIA names decode every glyph for keyboards and screen readers, every time.
