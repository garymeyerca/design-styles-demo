Three Rules, One Garden

The Acanthus order is restraint — ornament that grows from structure.

Rule I

Carved, not printed

Surfaces are hewn from one warm cream. Depth comes from soft relief — a gentle highlight above, a shadow pressed below — never flat blocks of colour.

Rule II

Symmetrical foliage

Acanthus leaves scroll outward in mirrored pairs. Vines frame, capitals crown, and every ornament answers another across an axis.

Rule III

Gilded with intent

Gold marks only what matters — the active, the chosen, the focused. Green and earth carry the rest, calm and serene.
