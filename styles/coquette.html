the secret recipe

Three rules, one feeling

Coquette is romance — tenderness dressed in ribbon.

one
Rule 01

A blushing palette

Soft pinks, cream and butter yellow with pearl whites. Nothing harsh — colour should feel like a gentle blush.

two
Rule 02

Bows on everything

Ribbons, lace trim, scalloped edges, pearls and dainty hearts. Decoration is the whole point — pile it on, sweetly.

three
Rule 03

Dainty, romantic type

An elegant serif paired with a flowing script. Letters should look handwritten in a love letter, never typed on a memo.
