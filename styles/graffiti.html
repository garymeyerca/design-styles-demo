Crew Code

Three rules every writer on the wall lives by.

Rule 01

Outline Everything

Fat black keylines hold the colour in. Nothing on the wall floats — every shape is cut hard against the concrete.

Rule 02

Let It Drip

Real spray runs. Drips, splatter and overspray are not mistakes — they are the proof of the hand.

Rule 03

Go Loud

Vivid caps, tilted panels, taped stencils. Restraint gets buffed — a real piece demands the eye.
