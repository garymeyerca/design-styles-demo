Questions From the Subconscious

A movement that frees the imagination by mining the unconscious — pairing photoreal craft with impossible juxtapositions, so a familiar object becomes a strange revelation.

Three rules of the dream

Surrealism is craft in service of the impossible.

Rule 01

Impossible juxtaposition

Place the familiar where it cannot belong — a clock that melts, an egg in the sky. Meaning is born from the collision.

Rule 02

Precise, elegant craft

The stranger the idea, the steadier the hand. Long dramatic shadows and fine rendering make the impossible feel true.

Rule 03

Uncanny, never chaotic

A dream has its own quiet order. Tilt, float and melt with restraint — unsettle the eye without ever confusing it.
