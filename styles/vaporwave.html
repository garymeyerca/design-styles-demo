They are signifiers of "high culture" and "the exotic" lifted out of context — a deliberate, surreal collage that feels both luxurious and hollow at once.

Three rules, one mood

Rule 01 第一

Gradient horizons

Everything bathes in a single pink-to-purple sky. Pastels stay washed-out and slightly faded, like a sun-bleached postcard.

Rule 02 第二

Glitch & collage
