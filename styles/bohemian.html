The Weaver's Creed

Three threads, one feeling

Bohemian design is abundance — gathered, layered, and lived-in.

Thread 01
i.

Jewel & earth together

Burnt orange, deep teal, mustard and plum settle over a terracotta-cream ground — rich, warm, never timid.

Thread 02
ii.

Pattern upon pattern

Mandala, paisley, ethnic geometry and woven warp layer freely — eclectic texture is the point, not the noise.

Thread 03
iii.

Handcrafted & well-travelled

Stitched edges, organic radii and characterful serifs make every surface feel gathered on a long, happy journey.
