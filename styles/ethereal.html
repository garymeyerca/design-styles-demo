Three rules, one feeling

Ethereal design is lightness — presence from the faintest possible touch.

Rule 01

Translucent veils

Surfaces are frosted glass, not solid panels — colour drifts through from the washes behind, so nothing ever feels heavy.

Rule 02

Feathered shadows

No crisp drop shadows. Depth is a soft exhale — wide, blurred and pale, like mist gathering beneath a cloud.

Rule 03

Slow, drifting motion

Everything moves as if underwater — long, gentle easing that breathes rather than snaps, and never demands attention.
