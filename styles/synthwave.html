Three rules, one neon night

Synthwave is mood — everything earns its glow.

Rule 01

Everything glows

Neon text-shadow on headings, box-shadow halos on borders and controls. Light leaks from every edge — the night is never truly dark.

Rule 02

Endless horizon

A perspective grid recedes to a vanishing point, a striped sun sets behind it. Depth comes from the road, not the shadow.

Rule 03

CRT texture

Scanlines, chromatic glitch and a vignette sell the cathode-ray fantasy — a screen that hums with 1980s electricity.
