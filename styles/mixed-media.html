the method

Three rules of the paste-up

Mixed media is abundance — many materials, one surface.

Rule 01
01

Layer everything

Paper on tape on newsprint on paint. Depth comes from real overlap and drop shadows — every scrap sits on top of another.

Rule 02
02

Never align

Tilt the scraps. Torn edges, slight rotations and crooked tape read as handmade — a perfect grid would kill it.

Rule 03
03

Mash the fonts

A serif headline, a condensed sans, a felt-tip scrawl — clashing typefaces are the whole point, like cut-out ransom letters.
