Our Craft

Three rules, one cozy feeling

Scrapbooking is warmth — every layer placed by hand.

Rule 01

Layer with paper & tape

Patterned paper, journaling cards, washi tape and photo corners stack up so the page feels physically built, not flatly printed.

Rule 02

Tilt everything a touch

Nothing sits perfectly square. A degree or two of rotation on each card and label reads as a real human hand, not a CSS grid.

Rule 03

Label it by hand

Handwritten captions and embossed dymo tape do the talking. Warm sepia ink, friendly serifs and a marker hand keep it personal.
