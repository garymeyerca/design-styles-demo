Three Rules of the Sea

Nautical design is order — sturdy, charted and shipshape.

Rule 01

Navy & white

A deep hull navy against crisp sailcloth white, with a single bold red ensign for every accent. High contrast, no fog.

Rule 02

Brass & rope

Warm brass rivets and twisted rope trim soften the hull — fittings you could grip, ornament with a working purpose.

Rule 03

Shipshape edges

Squared corners, firm borders and chunky drop-shadows. Every panel fastened down hard — stowed and squared away.
