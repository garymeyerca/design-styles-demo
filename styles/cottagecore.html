Three little ways of ours

Cottagecore is gentleness — charm gathered slowly, by hand.

Way the First

Made by hand

Nothing is machine-perfect. Slightly wonky corners, dashed stitches and hand-lettered labels keep everything feeling personal and warm.

Way the Second

Grown from nature

Sage green, butter cream, barn red and warm wood — every colour borrowed straight from a garden, a pantry or a sunlit field.

Way the Third

Cosy & unhurried

Soft serifs, gentle sways and gingham checks. Motion is slow and kind — nothing here is ever in a rush.
