Three rules, one glow

Aurora is atmosphere — depth from light, motion that drifts.

Rule 01

A night-sky canvas

Every page rests on deep indigo void, scattered with stars. Light only matters because the darkness behind it is total.

Rule 02

Drifting borealis light

Soft-blurred ribbons of teal, violet and pink sweep across the sky in slow, organic motion — never still, never hurried.

Rule 03

Translucent, iridescent glass

Panels are frosted and weightless, edged with a shimmering spectral border — so the aurora glows softly through everything.
